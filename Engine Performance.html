indicated power(i.p)

its the actual rate of work done by the working fluid pn the piston , in other words The total power developed by combustion of fuel in the combustion chamber is called Indicated Power (IP).
It can be calculated as:

I.P = P mLAN
  • N=number of cylinders
  • Pmi=Indicated mean effective pressure,(bars)
  • L=Length of stroke,(m)
  • A=Area of piston,(m2)
  • K = for 4 stroke engine

Brake power(b.p)

this is the measured output of the engine.it is usually by a power absorpation device such as a brake or dyanamometer which can in such a way that yhe torque exerted by the engine can be measured .

The power is given by: b.p = 2πNT where T is torque
In case of prony brake, brake power of the engine, where
  • W=Brake load in newtons.
  • l=Length of arm in metres.
  • N=Speed of the engine in r.p.m.
In case of rope brake, brake power of the engine, where :
  • W=Dead load in newtons.
  • S=Spring balance reading in newtons.
  • D=Diameter of the brake drum in metres.
  • d = Diameter of the rope in metres.
  • N=Speed of the engine in r.p.m.

Friction power (f.p) and mechanical efficincy:

Friction power: f.p = i.p - b.p . the mechanical effciency of the engine is: η_m = b.p \ i.p

indicated mean effective pressure (imep)

imep = net area of the indicator digram \ swept volume x indicated scale .

break mean effective pressure and break thermal efficincy.

the overall efficiency of the engine is given by the brake thermal effciency η_BT = brake power \ engine supplied. η_BT= b.p \ mf * Qnet

specific fuel consumption

it is the mass of fuel consumed per unit power output per hour , and is a criterion of economic power production sfc = mf\ b.p
low value of s.f.c are obviously desired .typical best value of bsfc for SI engines are about 270g\kw.h and for c.i engines are about 200g\kw.h

indicated thermal effciency.

Indicated thermal efficiency is the kind of engine thermal efficiency that is given by the ratio of indicated power generated by the engine to the power generated by the combustion of the fuel. It states the amount of power taken by the piston out of the total fuel power. The indicated thermal efficiency is denoted by the symbol η_IT
η_IT= Indicated power \ Fuel power

volumetric effciency

Volumetric Efficiency is only used with four_stroke cycle engine. It is defined as the ratio of the volume if air induced, measured at the free air conditions, to the swept volume of the cyliner. η_s = v / v_s.
